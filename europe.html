    Germany

    Germany is fast becoming one of the most favoured choices for international education with hundreds of thousands of students seeking an entry in German universities. This country offers an unparalleled learning ecosystem known for producing high quality graduates who achieve high employability and better salaries in the global job market.

    • More than 400 state-recognised institutions of higher education
    • 20,000 different study programmes to choose from
    • Germany boasts of having over 400,000 international students
    • Post Study Work Visa up to 1.5 Years
    • Study in Europe's strongest economy

    Popular Courses in Germany

    • Engineering & Technology
    • Computer Science & Information Technology
    • Business & Management
    • Creative Arts & Humanities
    • Social Sciences

    Malta

    Malta has a rich history that dates to prehistory, with a modern outlook that has kept pace with the world. International students who choose to study in Malta are guaranteed quality education with world recognition. The country offers a unique blend of Mediterranean culture and English-language education in the heart of Europe.

    • Top-notch quality of education at a reasonable cost
    • Plethora of opportunities for career-growth
    • 90% of the population speaks English
    • Popular destination for English language courses in Europe
    • More than 200 courses offered across various disciplines

    Popular Courses in Malta

    • Medicine
    • Business and Management
    • Computer Science & IT
    • Tourism and Hospitality
    • Education & Teaching

    Austria

    Austria offers considerable cultural diversity and depth, as well as exceptional academic and social exposure for international students. The country has a thriving industry base and high employment ratio, and is bordered by eight distinct countries that are easily accessible through Austria's effective rail system.

    • Well-respected universities and higher education facilities worldwide
    • Thriving industry base with high employment ratio for graduates
    • Recognized as one of the most livable countries in the world
    • Seven Austrian universities in QS World University Rankings
    • Various scholarship opportunities for international students

    Popular Courses in Austria

    • Business & Management
    • Computer Science & IT
    • Engineering & Technology
    • Environmental Studies
    • Social Sciences

    Poland

    Poland has emerged as an attractive destination for international students, offering high-quality education at affordable costs. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and growing economy, Polish universities are gaining recognition worldwide, particularly in fields like medicine, engineering, and business.

    • Over 400 higher education institutions, including prestigious universities dating back to 1364
    • More than 800 programs taught entirely in English
    • Home to more than 85,000 international students
    • Affordable tuition fees and low cost of living compared to Western Europe
    • Opportunity to study in the heart of Europe with Schengen visa benefits

    Popular Courses in Poland

    • Medicine & Dentistry
    • Engineering & Technology
    • Business & Economics
    • Computer Science
    • Architecture

    Slovakia

    Slovakia is emerging as an attractive destination for international students, offering high-quality education at affordable costs. With its central European location, rich cultural heritage, and growing number of English-taught programs, Slovakia provides excellent opportunities for students seeking quality education in the heart of Europe.

    • Over 15,000 international students studying in Slovakia

    Greece

    Greece, the birthplace of Western civilization, is emerging as an attractive destination for international students seeking quality education in a historically rich environment. With its affordable tuition fees and Mediterranean lifestyle, Greece offers a unique blend of academic excellence and cultural immersion.

    • Wide range of programs in English, especially at postgraduate level

    • Affordable tuition fees compared to other EU countries
    • Opportunity to study in a country with rich history and beautiful landscapes
